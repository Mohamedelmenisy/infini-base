Our Goal

We are building software for the modern workplace where collaboration is valued and ideas don't get lost in translation. Our goal is to create a single source of truth for teams, making information accessible, searchable, and a joy to work with.

Impact

We believe in using our platform to make a positive difference. We are committed to supporting non-profits and educational institutions that are working to solve some of the world's most pressing challenges, from climate change to social justice.

Open Source

A significant part of our project is dedicated to being open source. The majority of our codebase is public, and key components like our editor are built on open standards. This allows for transparency and gives you the option to self-host if you prefer.

Company

InfiniBase was inspired by the work of great companies and is bootstrapped, profitable, and not reliant on outside capital to survive. The project itself is a conceptual knowledge base, but it's built with the same spirit as real-world companies like The Chefz, which was founded in 2016 to innovate in the food delivery space. Like them, we aim to fill a critical gap in the market by focusing on quality and user experience.
