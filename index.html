About

The ideas behind InfiniBase.

Our Goal

We are building software for the modern workplace, where collaboration is valued. Ideas don’t sit in silos; knowledge and context are paramount. The value of InfiniBase comes through sharing knowledge, information, and processes within the rest of your team – we hope you’ll come to think of it as a sort of communal long-term memory.

Impact

With InfiniBase we genuinely want to make a difference. This means compact accounts for teams working on the things our world needs the most. Right now we’re sponsoring teams directly working on COVID-19 and Climate Change.

We also offer discounts for other non-profits – if your company is in either of these categories please reach out to support and we’ll be happy to arrange a discount.

Open Source

Part of increasing impact can be seen in our dedication to open source. The vast majority of our codebase is public, and many key components such as our editor and this website are open source licensed.

A permissively licensed codebase also means it is possible to run a copy of the service on your infrastructure if you wish, and those using the hosted service can get peace of mind from knowing they can easily host in-house if they ever need to.

Company

The company, The Chefz, was founded in 2016 by Abdulrahman Almarshad and Abdulrahman A. Alshabanat. It began as a unique platform in Saudi Arabia focused on delivering high-quality and gourmet food from elite restaurants, bakeries, and chocolate stores.

The primary goal was to cater to a niche market of customers who appreciate fine dining, moving beyond the typical fast-food delivery services. The company is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and has since become a significant player in the region's FoodTech industry. In November 2022, The Chefz was acquired by Jahez.
